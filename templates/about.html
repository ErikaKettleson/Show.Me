Above is a look from Valentino's Fall 2014 show and the colors returned by the color algorithm. My k-means analysis sampled 4 color clusters on a pre-processed, cropped version of each runway image.






I used a k-means clustering algorithm to pull the top four colors out of each of the 40-100 looks that make up a runway show. I analyzed about 20,000 looks from 600 runway shows from Fall 2013 through Spring 2017, gathering nearly 6000 data points tracking colors segmented by year, season and brand.

I calculated the top ten colors from every show after aggregating the individual looks by color frequency. Below is a selection from Gucci's Spring 2016 show beside it's color palette of top colors.
